NUCKS1

Nuclear ubiquitous casein and cyclin-dependent kinase substrate 1 (P1)

NUCKS1 ❯ Q9H1E3

Gene names

NUCKS1 NUCKS JC7

Function

FUNCTION: Chromatin-associated protein involved in DNA repair by promoting homologous recombination (HR) (PubMed:26323318). Binds double-stranded DNA (dsDNA) and secondary DNA structures, such as D-loop structures, but with less affinity than RAD51AP1 (PubMed:26323318). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:26323318}.

Keywords
