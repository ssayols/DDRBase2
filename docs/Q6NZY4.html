ZCCHC8

Zinc finger CCHC domain-containing protein 8 (TRAMP-like complex RNA-binding factor ZCCHC8)

ZCCHC8 Q6NZY4

Gene names

ZCCHC8

Function

FUNCTION: Scaffolding subunit of the trimeric nuclear exosome targeting (NEXT) complex that is involved in the surveillance and turnover of aberrant transcripts and non-coding RNAs (PubMed:27871484). NEXT functions as an RNA exosome cofactor that directs a subset of non-coding short-lived RNAs for exosomal degradation. May be involved in pre-mRNA splicing (Probable). It is required for 3-end maturation of telomerase RNA component (TERC), TERC 3-end targeting to the nuclear RNA exosome, and for telomerase function (PubMed:31488579). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:27871484, ECO:0000269|PubMed:31488579, ECO:0000305|PubMed:16263084}.
